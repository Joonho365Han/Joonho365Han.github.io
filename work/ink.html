INK

Ink is a mobile notebook application. The goal of Ink is to make categorizing and taking notes as simple as possible. As a person who takes notes frequently, I wanted a way to quickly add new notes and organize them in the simplest way possible. Many notebook apps had different "modes" for categorizing and looking at the archive, which actually confused me because it was like treating notes as digital objects: notes teleporting between screens, automatically reading and setting alarms from the notes, too many tabs that have different functions, etc. I just wanted to treat notes like they are physical papers. I wanted a singular "home" button that made me clear where to look at when I want to see my notes. I wanted to slide the pages as if I am physically sliding through a pile of notes. I wanted a singular way of organizing my notes so that I know exactly where to go to find them. In other words, I wanted to be "spacially aware" of my notes. And none of the available apps allowed me to do that.
