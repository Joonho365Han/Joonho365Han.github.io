INK

The goal of Ink is to make categorizing and taking notes simple. As a person who takes notes frequently, I wanted a way to quickly add new notes and organize them in the simplest way possible. Many notebook apps tend to have different "modes" for categorizing and looking at the archive, which actually confused me because I wasn't used to treating notes like company data: notes jumping around, setting alarms depending on contents of the notes, too many tabs that has different functions. I just wanted to treat notes like they are physical papers. I wanted a singular "home" button that made me clear where to look at when I want to see my notes. I wanted to slide the pages as if I am physically sliding through a pile of notes.
