Seltra

Seltra was a personal project aiming to create the most beautiful wristband. I believe true power, the power that is fair and brings people happiness, is beautiful. Although this statement doesn't work the other way around, I thought that if I create the most beautiful piece of art, I'll feel powerful.

Over the course of 7 years, Seltra changed form. As a child, all I did was to simply decorate a crayon's wrapping paper and play with the crayon as a laser shooter. Gradually, I learned how to sculpt, woodwork, and learn how to value light. Seltra represents the change in my ideology more than my skills as an artist of an engineer. The basic idea that I learned through making Seltra is "do not give up." No matter how busy I am, how hard anything seems, and whether it seems impossible to do it myself, all it takes is a tiny bit of effort day by day that makes things happen. Seltra was the crystallization of that statement, especially since the very first wristband-style version was created during the toughest time of my life: during my junior high in Korea.
