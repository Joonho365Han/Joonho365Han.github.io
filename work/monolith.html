MONOLITH

Monolith is a mobile archive of ancient weapons and history of the Epicross Universe. The Epicross Universe is a fictional background my novel Seltra takes place in (Seltra is also a title of my other project). In this world, a rogue martial artist Raid Cleric and his comrades handle hundreds of different weapons in a continuous fight. Monolith combines my novel with another book, Archive of Weapons, modernizing the entire archive making it physically indestructible.
