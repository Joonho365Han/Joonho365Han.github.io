Around the summer of 2014, I started an interview series aiming to determine what makes people who they are. This is an ongoing project. who knows? Maybe one day this page will be filled with people who are truly themselves and show on camera what makes people people.

Motivation

After coming to America, I wandered around looking for ways to fit in. Later I realized that in this country, there is no "common way," and I must find my own way of life. I thought looking into the lives of people who I know well would help me understand what guidelines people follow in their lives and how I should create my own.
