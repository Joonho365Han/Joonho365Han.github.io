LISTEN

Listen is a series of moments recorded when the interviewees are the most themselves. The style of initial test videos shown below are inspired by Lynn Hirschberg's Screen Tests. Progressively, the series will evolve from this style and hopefully present each interviewee from diverse angles. The intent of this work is to learn how people can feel empathy despite the growing diversity and generational gaps in the Unites States.

Motivation

Starting high school, I came to the states eager to live a different life. I decided to throw myself away and learn how everybody else behaved so I could fit in. Years later, I realized I am beautiful for simply being myself, but I had forgotten about who I am. I needed a guideline to define myself as a person. I started interviewing others hoping to learn how others achieved this and to learn from them.

Today, I have a better sense of who I am, but the way people look at themselves still interests me. More through time, I realize how diverse America is. In other words, there was no true "normal" person. I became fascinated by how so many people in this country could relate to each other without a definition "normality." From this moment, "Listen" changed its goal.
