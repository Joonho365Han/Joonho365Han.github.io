Motivation

Starting high school, I came to the states eager to live a different life. I decided to throw myself away and learn how everybody else behaved so I could fit in. Years later, I realized I am beautiful for simply being myself, but I had forgotten about who I am. I needed to learn how to define myself as a person, and decided to look at how others did so.

Today, I have a better sense of who I am, but how people define themselves still interests me. More through time, I realize how diverse America is. In other words, there was no true "normal" person. I became fascinated by how so many people in this country could relate to each other without a definition "normality." Thus, "Listen" now tries to film people and find what part of human do people understand each other.
