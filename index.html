This website is not me.

You can never define a human, at least using a website, because humans grow and change.


This website is not a resume.

It's an archive of my thoughts and ideas so that maybe if somebody is interested my ideas can be shared, discussed, and improved upon; not to market myself.


This website is not art.

It's a work of design that serves to present my ideas eloquently, not to embellish my work beyond what it is.


This website is not a curation.

It's a collage of everything I am without judgement, and thus has no theme besides "Joonho Han's Ideas". It should also be the only place on the web that collectively shows my ideas.
