JOONHO HAN

I am a creator, serial tinkerer, and child at heart.

I love making my own toy, be it a painting, sculpture, or electronics.

Unlike what many say, it was never because I "needed something of my own." One day, I just had a strong urge to bring my ideas to life, and it simply has continued until today.
