This is an art gallery website. Artist Joonho Han posts his works here.

Realizing my passion for art was a bit too late, but I am still trying hard to bring back the creativity deep within me. I believe the world is a better place with the symbiosis of technology and art. One day, I'll find a career that achieves this. One day...

This website is my gallery and about what I create when ever possible. The main motivation for my creation is the pursuit of ultimate beauty. Through time, the definition of my beauty gradually included the view points of other people and balance with the society. I plan to maintain this status through time.
