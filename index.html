Preface

NO COMPROMISE FOR A DREAM

I was raised as a very independent child. My parents were very supportive of my desire to paint, sculpt, and draw.

Realizing my passion for art was a bit too late, but I am still trying hard to bring back the creativity deep within me. I believe the world is a better place with the symbiosis of technology and art. One day, I'll find a career that achieves this. Check out my resume here.

This website is my gallery and about what I create when ever possible. The main motivation for my creation is the pursuit of ultimate beauty. Through time, the definition of my beauty gradually included the view points of other people and balance with the society. I plan to maintain this status through time.
