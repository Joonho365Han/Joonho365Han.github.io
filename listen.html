LISTEN

Listen is a series of interviews in which I try to capture each individual's "human beauty." It is difficult to explain human beauty, but it's similar to being approachable. Part of the goal for this interview is to bring out the hidden quirk within the interviewee, so hopefully he/she has something of his/her own after they leave.

Motivation

Do you value your virtues? When I was young, the only thing I cared was to become part of the crowd. Virtues for me were only tools to become accepted by the masses, just so I do not feel left out. This view made me unrespectable. I lost friends, became lonely, and finally started to think, "becoming a part of the crowd is not a big enough of a reason to live." I searched for my own meaning of life, and I started this interview series to peek into other people's memories; I wanted to learn from the paths the others took. Years later, I would found my own meaning of life, but other people's path of life still intrigues me today.
