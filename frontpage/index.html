"DO DILIGENCE"

Here's a story.

I thought Do Diligence was an American idiom. I remember hearing something that sounded similar, and I thought "Hmm, that must be spelled 'Do Diligence.'" I was raised in Korea, a goal-oriented society, but my parents made sure I valued the process as much as the result. So when I heard this 'idiom,' I thought "It must mean to be diligent no matter what. Wow. Persistence must be a universal trait. I should stick to it." Ironically, the phrase I actually heard was "Due Diligence" which means something completely different. But, thanks to this coincidence, I grew up believing that a lot can be achieved when you just persist through.

Seltra

This paragraph will be about Seltra. Seltra was a personal project aiming to create the most beautiful wristband. I believe true power, the power that is fair and brings people happiness, is beautiful. Although this statement doesn't work the other way around, I believe that if I create the most beautiful piece of art, I'll feel powerful.

Listen

Listen is a video series trying to capture the core values of an interviewee: who they are deep down, what virtues they believe in, what guidelines they follow in action. The interview series was created to find the guidance of life through others' lives in times of identity crisis.

Monolith

Monolith, an Android app, is an archive of ancient weapons and history of the Epicross Universe. Since a very young age, Joonho created his own universe and wrote a novel on one of the biggest wars that took place in it. Monolith is a mobile replacement for the past sketches and paperback books he wrote about what existed in that world.
