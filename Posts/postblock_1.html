Feb.19.2017

Concept: Raspberry pi cluster that is just better. What do I mean by better? I mean that it won't be just multiple pi's stacked up one another by spacers. There will be an aluminum casing that will also act as the heat sink. The Pi 3s will be thinned down so they can be closely stacked on one another. Power will come from a dedicated power circuit, which removes the need for an external power block. And the coolest of all, they will be a GPU cluster.
