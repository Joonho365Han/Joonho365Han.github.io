DO DILIGENCE

A wristwatch shaped wristband that has a jewelry inside. It's pretty damn cool considering that nobody thinks of breaking down a watch or having a memento inside a cask on their wrist. Here's a story. I thought Do Diligence was an American idiom. I remember hearing the phrase once, and I thought "Hmm, that must be spelled 'Do Diligence.' Wow. Such a cool idiom." Growing up under not-so-conservative parents, I learned to value the process as much as the result. So when I heard this 'idiom,' I thought "Wow. persistence muct be a universal trait. I guess I must embrace it." Ironically, the phrase I actually heard was "Due Diligence" which means something completely different. But, thanks to this coincidence, I was able to grow up believing that many things can be done with persistence.
