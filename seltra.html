Introduction

Seltra refers to the idea of the most beautiful object in the world and my attempts to realize it. There were a total of 4 attempts, starting 2008. After my last attempt, I concluded that it's impossible to finalize the object and turned it into a tattoo to carry its significance through my life.

Significance

The first meaning is, "Do not give up." When I was young, people around me believed art was unnecessary in life and sometimes stole my creations to hide them. I tried my best not to mind them and believe in myself. So when I successfully created my first rendition of Seltra, I felt very proud and realized that no matter how hard anything seems, what you believe in will determine the outcome. The second meaning is, "Everything will change." I determined the project will never end because I will grow and change as a person and so will my definition of beauty. Accepting that even what I believed to be perfect eventually will not be the same made realize that everything will change.
